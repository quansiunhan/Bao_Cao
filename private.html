PRIVACY AND COOKIE POLICY

1. What personal information do we collect and hold about you?

The information we request from you will include the following:

Your name and address and other contact information.

Details of your purchases.

Your billing information.

We only collect information that is needed by us to provide the online services that we offer. You are not required to give us any personal information, which may be requested. However, if you do not, it may affect our ability to provide you with our products or services.

2. How does Matthews Liquor collect personal information?

Matthews Liquor collects personal information directly from you when you submit it either in-store, online, or by telephone. We may also collect information from third parties or public sources, such as names, telephone numbers, email, addresses and/or social media profile details sourced from organisations with the permission to release this information.

3. Use and disclosure of your personal information

We may use your personal information to do certain things, including to:

Undertake actions on your behalf.

Administer and manage the services we provide you.

Provide you with information about other products or services that may be of benefit to you; and

Facilitate our internal business operations, including fulfilment of any legal requirements.

We may be required to disclose personal information to various parties, including but not limited to:

To our agents, contractors, or third-party service providers that provide financial, administrative, or other services in connection with the operation of our business; or

Administer and manage the services we provide you.

Where the law requires or permits us to do so (e.g. to law enforcement agencies).

By supplying us your information, you have given us permission to communicate to you via mail, e-mail, MMS, SMS, social media, and telephone, regarding our products and services, for an indefinite period.

4. Information security

We take all reasonable steps to protect all personal or company information from misuse, loss, unauthorized access, modification, or disclosure.

Some information is kept for several years to comply with legal requirements. Any personal information that is no longer needed is destroyed.

5. Access and Correction

You can request access at any time to personal information that we hold. We will process your request within a reasonable amount time. You may ask us at any time to correct personal information held by us about you which you believe is incorrect or out-of-date.

We aim to ensure that all information we hole, collect, use or disclose is accurate, complete, and up-to-date. You must promptly notify us if there are any changes to your personal information that are needed.

6. Changes to the Matthews Liquor Group Privacy Policy

Matthews liquor reserve the right to make changes and amendments to privacy policy. Any changes made to our policy(s) will be update on our website within a reasonable amount of time, being no longer than 24 hours.
